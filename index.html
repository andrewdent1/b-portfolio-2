Grow With Grace

Digital Marketing & Social Media Services

Planting the Seeds for Authentic Growth

Hi there! I’m Brenna Grace, founder of Grow With Grace LLC and a passionate digital marketing professional with over five years of experience in the ever-changing world of social media. Since 2019, I’ve been dedicated to helping brands not only establish an online presence but also build authentic, lasting connections with their audiences while achieving sustainable, long-term growth.

When I launched Grow With Grace in 2022, my mission became clear: to empower small businesses to thrive in the digital space while giving owners the freedom to focus on what they do best. I believe that marketing should be a tool for connection, not confusion. By handling the complexities of digital strategy, I aim to give business owners peace of mind, knowing their brand is in good hands.

For me, growth is about more than just numbers. It’s about fostering real connections, embracing continuous learning, and creating something meaningful that resonates with your audience. My passion for exploration plays a huge role in how I approach every project. Whether it’s studying abroad in Italy, traveling across the U.S. in a converted van, or immersing myself in different cultures, these experiences have shaped my perspective on life and business. They’ve taught me the importance of adaptability, creativity, and authenticity, allowing me to transfer these qualities into my work.

When I’m not helping brands shine online, you can usually find me planning my next big adventure, soaking up the outdoors, or brainstorming innovative, creative ways to help businesses grow and stand out in their industry. I thrive on collaborating with those who are eager to tell their stories, reach their audiences, and make a lasting impact.

WHAT I DO

Digital Marketing

Connecting brands with audiences online through strategies like social media, SEO, and email campaigns are my strong suits. It’s essential for brand awareness, allowing you to track engagement and adapt campaigns in real time.

Social Media Management

Creating, scheduling, and monitoring content across platforms is the basis of social media management. Building an online presence for your brand is the #1 goal of utilizing social media. It’s crucial for brand awareness, allowing you to connect directly with your target audience, respond to feedback, and foster a loyal community.

Content Creation

Crafting engaging and relevant materials that captivate target audiences across various platforms. From writing articles to producing videos and graphics,my goal is to deliver high-quality content that aligns with your brand’s voice.
